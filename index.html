Game Concept

Stonefield focuses on building a growing settlement across natural terrain.
Players place foundations, connect routes between structures, and manage how each new building contributes to expansion.
Where you put your buildings really matters - it can make or break how well your settlement develops. Things get trickier as you build more - you'll need to be smarter about planning your space.

Gameplay Structure
